## Step 2 — Add a DNS record (5 minutes)

This connects a subdomain of your choice to your page — for example `join.yourdomain.com`, `apply.yourdomain.com`, or any keyword you like.

Log into wherever you registered your domain — usually GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, or a similar registrar. Once logged in, look for **DNS Settings** or **DNS Management** under your domain. Add the following record:

| Type | Name / Host | Value / Target | TTL |
|------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| CNAME | `join` *(or your chosen keyword)* | `cname.vercel-dns.com` | Auto |

**The Name / Host field** is the subdomain prefix — the word that appears before your domain. You can use anything: `join`, `apply`, `start`, `get`, etc. Just let us know what you chose so we can match it on our end.

Once added, your page will go live within a few minutes. No further action needed on your end — we handle the rest.

**Not sure where to find DNS settings?** Just tell us who your domain registrar is and we'll send you a step-by-step screenshot guide for that specific platform.
